Game night, Raid night

By: Nocturne Saint


My heart is pumping, The ground is rumbling
My spirit is ready to fight.

Battle cry, the morale is high
my comrades are yelling with might

Buffs from the support, The dps report
The tanks have entered the fray.

This may be a game, as we lay claim
on riches this night of the raid.









Eat. Sleep. Game. Repeat.
